About

Hi, I'm Kelly Selcher! I am a front-end web developer who creates stunning, unforgettable responsive websites in Middletown, PA.

I am enthusiastic about integrating creativity into my career. With a background in the Liberal Arts, I first encountered website design while taking some noncredit college courses through Florida State University and the University of Massachusetts, Lowell. Over time, I decided on website design as a career since it combines artistry with the practicality of STEM. I enrolled in a master’s degree program at Southern New Hampshire University in Information Technology with a concentration in website design. I developed an interest in search engine optimization after basing my master’s thesis on it. After graduation, I pursued further studies in coding and website design.

When not coding, you will find me researching my family tree, crafting creative writing pieces, and reading a book.
